KMC, Inc. Cookie Policy

Overview​

KMC, Inc. respects your privacy and is committed to transparent practices. This Cookie Policy applies to our websites, branded pages on third-party platforms, and applications accessed through these platforms, operated by or on behalf of KMC, Inc.

Use of Cookies​

We use cookies, tags, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to personalize your experience, understand usage patterns, and improve and secure our sites. These technologies are used for various purposes:

  • Absolutely Necessary Cookies: Essential for navigating and using features on our sites.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect aggregate information on site usage to enhance user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember user preferences for a more personalized experience.

We may also use third-party service performance and retargeting cookies for behavioral advertising and effectiveness tracking.

Your Choices​

You have control over cookie usage through your browser settings. Rejecting cookies may restrict access to certain functionalities. Learn more about managing cookies and opting out of behavioral advertising at various digital advertising platforms.

Consent and Changes​

By using our sites, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. We may update this policy periodically, and changes will be posted on our website.

Contact Information​

For questions regarding this Cookie Policy, please contact us at cai@kmccorp.com or call +1 (703) 585-9233.
